# Product

## Product category

CallForce is an AI Omnichannel Sales & Support Platform.

It is not just a chatbot builder. The target product replaces or amplifies human work in:

- inbound calls;
- outbound calls;
- web chat;
- messengers and social channels;
- lead qualification;
- bookings/orders;
- CRM updates;
- missed lead recovery;
- customer support;
- quality control and analytics.

## Core AI agent roles

- AI receptionist: accepts requests and books customers.
- AI sales agent: qualifies, sells and follows up.
- AI support agent: answers questions and resolves issues.
- AI call center agent: handles inbound/outbound phone calls.
- AI lead recovery agent: returns missed leads.
- AI CRM assistant: updates deals, statuses and tasks.
- AI analytics agent: shows lost money and growth opportunities.
- AI supervisor: checks call/chat quality.

## Onboarding flow

1. Create company.
2. Choose niche.
3. Create AI agent from a template.
4. Add knowledge.
5. Connect channel.
6. Test dialogue/call.
7. Publish.
8. Monitor leads and conversations.

## Production promise

The product should promise outcomes only when proven:

- faster response;
- fewer missed leads;
- more bookings/orders;
- lower support load;
- better quality control;
- measurable recovered revenue.

Do not promise real autonomous calling, payments or compliance until those paths are tested with real providers and documented evidence.
